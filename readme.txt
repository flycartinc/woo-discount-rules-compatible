2) Multi Currency for WooCommerce By villatheme - https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-multi-currency/

3) WooCommerce Wholesale Prices By Rymera Web Co - https://wholesalesuiteplugin.com/woocommerce-wholesale-prices-premium/

4) Product Add-Ons By Woocommerce - https://woocommerce.com/products/product-add-ons/

5) Product Bundles By SomewhereWarm - https://woocommerce.com/products/product-bundles/

6) Gravity Forms Product Add-ons By lucas stark - https://woocommerce.com/products/gravity-forms-add-ons/

7) WooCommerce Brands By Woocommerce - https://woocommerce.com/products/brands/

8) Perfect WooCommerce Brands By Alberto de Vera Sevilla - https://wordpress.org/plugins/perfect-woocommerce-brands/

9) Composite Products By Somewhere Warm - https://woocommerce.com/products/composite-products/

10) WC Role Based Price By Varun sridharan - https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-role-based-price/

11) Extra Product Options (Product Addons) for WooCommerce By themehigh - https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-extra-product-options/
